The Lee family

the Lee family is a traditional Chinese-Canadian family living in Toronto. They have strong cultural roots, and their lives revolve around maintaining their temple, which honors their ancestor Sun Yee.

Meilin "Mei" Lee

The 13-year-old protagonist who transforms into a giant red panda when experiencing strong emotions. She is energetic, ambitious, and torn between pleasing her strict mother and embracing her own interests.

Ming Lee

Mei’s overprotective and strict mother. She wants Mei to follow family traditions and has high expectations, often embarrassing Mei without realizing it. She also has her own history with turning into a red panda.

Jin Lee

Mei’s calm and supportive father. He’s quieter compared to Ming but plays a crucial role in helping Mei understand and accept her transformation.
